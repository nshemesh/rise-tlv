Who We Are

Rise is all about jumping high fives, Big FAT A+s and lifelong friendships. We connect mentors looking to make a change with kids eager to learn and grow. We began our journey in 2018 and since then we have changed countless lives. We are growing fast and alwyas looking for new mentors and mentees to join us.
